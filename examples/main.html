LikeLines prototype

This page showcases the LikeLines player heatmap navigation. The heatmap shows which bits of the video were found to be interesting by the community and allows you to jump to the interesting parts right away.

Videos:
JavaScript and Flash 8 are required to embed the YouTube player.

In the current prototype, the heatmaps are static and immutable. However, the key, innovative contribution of LikeLines is the collection and management of heatmap metadata, which can be applied in wide variety of use cases. The next version of the prototype will aggregate user input and generate heatmaps dynamically.

More information about LikeLines can be found here.
